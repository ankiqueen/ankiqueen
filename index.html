Why AnkiQueen?

We will remain free and open forever. All that our team asks is that you donate to the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society as we lost someone dear to our hearts to childhood leukemia. Thank you. ‚ù§

The AnkiQueen team utilizes the SuperMemo twenty rules for formulating knowledge to create high quality cards. This significantly reduces study time by:

  • Increasing overall retention per high quality card
  • Decreasing the number of cards needed to cover a topic

How You Can Help

Study Using Our Decks and Contribute Content

  1. Download a deck related to the subject you want to study (eg, USMLE Step 2 CK)
  2. Install the Appletto add-on to enable auto syncing. No setup is needed
  3. Optionally: Suggests edits to cards and/or even create new cards to be added to our decks
