Why AnkiQueen?

The AnkiQueen team utilizes the SuperMemo twenty rules for formulating knowledge to create high quality cards. This significantly reduces study time by:

  • Increasing overall retention per high quality card
  • Decreasing the number of cards needed to cover a topic
